# Code of Conduct

LegalEagle aims to be a respectful, useful, and careful open-source project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while staying clear about technical issues.
- Keep discussions focused on the project.
- Be careful with legal, personal, or sensitive information.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, abuse, or discriminatory language
- Sharing private legal documents or personal data without permission
- Publishing secrets, credentials, or exploit details in public issues
- Presenting generated legal output as professional legal advice

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and issues that violate this code. Repeated or severe violations may lead to restricted participation.
